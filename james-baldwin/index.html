James Baldwin, author

James Baldwin's examination in 1953 of the role of the Christian Church in the lives of African Americans, Go Tell It on the Mountain, is ranked as one of the best English-language novels of the 20th century.

Through his life, Baldwin befriended icons such as Marlon Brando, Malcolm X, Nina Simone, Martin Luther King, Maya Angelou, and Toni Morrison. Shortly before his death, he was given France's highest honor, La Légion D’Honneur.
