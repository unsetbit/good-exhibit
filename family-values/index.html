Children should be supported by those who love them. This is a family value.

Mothers and Fathers shouldn't have to live paycheck-to-paycheck in order to provide their kids a good life.

Child care, maternity leave, paternity leave, health care, and education are important family values issues that need to be better addressed in the United States.
